Ticket Seller Instructions

General Sales
  • Tickets are valid only for the location where they are purchased.
  • Help purchasers choose an available departure time.
  • Do not promise a different location or departure time.
  • Every passenger requires a physical ticket.
  • Children 2 years of age and under travel free, but still require a physical ticket.
  • Remind all passengers to arrive 20 minutes before their scheduled departure time.
  • Return transportation is included.
Financial Assistance
  • Financial assistance is available for those who cannot afford tickets, but it is not being widely advertised.
  • Do not ask for an explanation or proof.
  • Record via pen & paper the person's name and contact number, then issue the required tickets.
  • Record the assistance in the app or notes.
Volunteer Tickets
  • Issue the ticket for the location where it is being purchased.
  • Write "Volunteer" clearly on the physical ticket.
  • Select the appropriate "Volunteer" departure in the app.
  • Advise volunteers that the transportation model is still being finalized and may change.
  • If their departure location changes, the original ticket will still be honoured.
Volunteer Arrival
  • Early volunteer buses are planned to drop volunteers upstairs near the volunteer entrance.
  • Volunteers arriving later with the general Jamat, or through Jack Poole Plaza with family, may need to make their own way upstairs.
  • Guidance may be provided through signage, an AskMe volunteer or another support option. Whether this support will be inside or outside is still being confirmed.
  • All volunteers must report to Rooms 109–110 for check-in, registration and their volunteer identification card.
