Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Mulaqat Express?
Mulaqat Express is a ticketed shuttle model to help attendees travel in a more organized way between assigned Jamatkhanas, hubs, parking lots, the Premium Lounge Site, and the Didar venue.
2. How does the ticket work?
The digital ticketing system is the source of truth. Ticket details will be confirmed through the approved ticketing process and used to support boarding, capacity planning, and return transportation.
3. Does everyone need a Mulaqat Express ticket?
Yes. Each person travelling on the Mulaqat Express must have a ticket or be accounted for through the approved ticketing process. This helps support bus capacity, boarding, and return transportation planning.
4. Are tickets refundable or transferable?
No. Mulaqat Express tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable.
5. How much is the ticket?
Ticket pricing will be shared through official communications once confirmed.
