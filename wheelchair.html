Wheelchair-Accessible Taxi Transportation Option

Wheelchair-accessible taxi transportation will be available as an optional travel method for Jamati members who require wheelchair-accessible transportation to and from the venue.

Getting to the Venue
Passengers may contact a participating taxi provider that serves their pickup area. When booking, provide the company's Didar 26 Account ID and select one of the available arrival windows:
  • 5 a.m.
  • 6 a.m.
Bookings will be accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.

Leaving the Venue
Wheelchair-accessible taxis will be staged in the designated egress area following the programme. Return transportation will operate on a first-come, first-served basis. Passengers do not need to pre-book their return trip or use the same taxi company that provided their arrival trip.
